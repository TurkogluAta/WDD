Tips for Studying Online

Have a dedicated learning space

• When choosing a space, it better that space is separated from our entertainment zone or sleep area
•It is an advantage for us to use the same space for to attend lectures and examinations because searches state that we remember information better in the same environment we learn it in.
• Let others know that you are going to attend lectures, to reduce distractions from them.
